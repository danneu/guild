    The Bandits of Skyrim (OOC)

    The basic premise of this RP is that we play as a small group of bandits who have just formed. The year is 4E 201 and the Dovahkiin is roaming the land, killing dragons. The Thalmor are running around prosecuting Talos worshipers, Stormcloaks and Imperials are duking it out across the countryside.

    But that's all of little worry to you. You're a simple bandit, looking to make some less then legal cash. You've found a small group of fellow bandits hiding out in a cave between Riverwood and Whiterun. The group, known as the the Bleak Cliff Bandits, is run by a Imperial named Martin Caesar, a man with a reputation to be cunning and ruthless.

    You are the newest group to join the bandits group. After meeting at a predesignated location, you've been brought to the headquarters, located in an old mine. Here you'll meet your future "employees" and basically live life. But first lets see our newest recruits.


    Name:

    Race: Only one of the 10 races playable in the games: Breton, Imperial, Nord, Redguard, Altmer, Dunmer, Bosmer, Orc, Argonian, Khajiit

    Age: At least 18.

    Sex:

    Equipment: Nothing crazy here. You can use types of weapons outside of the games but the materials have to be lore friendly. Be realistic. A lowly bandits not going to have full dragon scale armor.

    Bio: Tell us a bit about your character. Just a bit about their personality and and their history.




    Name: Martin Caesar

    Race: Imperial

    Age: 31

    Sex: Male

    Equipment: Wears a full set of leather armor minus the helm. He has a silver sword he stole from some minor noble's house years ago and a small leather shield, both of which he is skilled with. Also carries a bow with 36 arrows, but rarely uses it.

    Bio: Born and raised in Chorrol in the Imperial Province, Martin was raised fairly well. His parents were traders of some small renown and they never went hungry. Martin received a decent education by private tutors and was expected to take over the family business. This all changed on his 19th birthday. A group of raiders came in one night and completely devastated the family's business. Wagons, animals, good... all destroyed. From then on Martin turned to a life of crime, believing that others should feel what he did. Independently, he went from pickpocket to thief, thief to cat burglar, burglar to assassin. He even did some informant work. Now he's "settled down" and is leading his own group of bandits.

    He's known for his cunning and ruthlessness, a very dangerous pair and the cause for him surviving so long on his own.
