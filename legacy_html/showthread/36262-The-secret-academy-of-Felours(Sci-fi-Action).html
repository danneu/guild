    The secret academy of Felours(Sci-fi/Action)

    (Explanations on shortenings and stuff like that below. This will be a college alike thing for the first period of the roleplay! Romance is therfor also allowed if ye want to. Please read the plot explanation before ragequitting! And please, if you find grammatical or spelling mistakes, TELL ME! I want to become better at English while having fun )

    On the planet earth, a new group with unknown purposes, are rising faster than they should. Gaining control of several secret and undiscovered places on earth, they started to need even more people, to feed their growth.

    But first things first... Humanity has spread throughout the galaxy, and have in total, somewhat settled on 27 different planets. The deomocracy is in ruins, and the UC have lost the most of their control on all of the planets under their control and supervision to the EF. The EF holds them in tight positions, where if they as much as move a muscle in the wrong direction the workers will be shot. Another group of rebels have taken up the war, but they are loosing their planets as well, faster than should be possible with the technology they have at the present time.

    But rebels are coming to get the EF, and before they know it...

    The secret organization have successfully kept a hidden acedemy, 500 feet below the earths surface for 10 years. Now what would an organization need an academy for?

    They need pilots, agents and soldiers to take the controls of different kinds of mechas and exoskeletons. They are building up manpower enough to destroy the UC from the inside, and they are using the other rebelling group as a distraction. But due to the UC's progress, they've made haste. They needed to take more people in at the academy than those who had the maximal potential. Several strange invites were send to people all over the world.

    The invite is a simple letter with a strange handwrote note in it.
    "We, the ones who will save this galaxy from the dangers to come, invite you to take the train or ferry. Pack your belongings, and wait outside your house at 12PM. Tell no one about it, or we won't be able to ensure their safety"

    Those who do agree, is escorted by a member from the secret organization, to either an underground trainstation, or to a small submarine. Once inside, they will be locked in a room with no windows, so that they can't tell where they are going. In other words- There is no turning back.

    Those who go by train, arrive in a big basement, where all the agents come and go from their daily lives, to their secret lives.

    Those who go by sea, will be forced to walk, unable to see the outside of the academy, to go inside the academy, through a big door. Once inside, is a big hall called "The welcoming hall"

    Now you have gotten an invite. will you take it? Will you leave it, and never take the chance of becoming a hero or a villain? will you go against the order when you grow up? Or will you go against the EF as you're supposed to?

    Explanations:
    Exoskeletion: A robotic suit, fitting tightly to the body, enabling the person inside to gain incredible athletic abbilities. that, and some even enable space travelling.
    Mecha: A robot controlled by a pilot like seen in many animes. (Just a bit more realistic size)
    UC: United colonies.
    EF: Earth forces.

    Story and plot explanations:
    You, we, all of us, are, or will become, students of the secret academy. Wether you're on of those whom have great potential, or those they took because they just needed you. The plot will advance with weekly events, that WILL affect your characters. In some cases you have to actively accept the difficulties, and in others you will be forced to join the event (Since invasions on the academy and stuff, arn't very easy to avoid~)

    this, however in the first 3 months, will be a RP in an academy, with both females AND males! So romance is definitly allowed! It might make part two even more intersting.

    Once a month, a year is about to end. In other words- You will only stay at the academy for three years to study.

    Once those three months end, the real deal will start. And the academy quickly will turn into your, or your enemies headquarters!

    The academy:

    Basement:
    The basement is a gigantic underground trainstation, where the secret society's teachers and so on, arrive in from their normal lives. Students are not allowed to be in the basement, and the security is difficult to get through. Not saying it's impossible though...

    First floor: The first floor is one gigantic hall, with a lot of tables and so on. This is used as the dinning hall, and the party hall, and the "welcome!" hall. There isn't a lot to describe here, except the nice little bar, and the high ammount of toilets.

    Second floor: Students quarters. Here you live, breathe, eat, kiss, make out- whatever you want to. As long as you don't injure anyone else, the secret organization couldn't care less. But if you do, it might not be very nice to be you...

    Third floor: The third floor is used for practicing. Classes takes place here. On this floor, you can find simulation rooms, and exoskeleton-traning modules. From there, you can also access the arena, through a long window-less hall. In other words, the third floor, is for traning you in using the machines.

    The Arena: The arena a fully interactive hall, where everything can be changed to any setting you want to. Sadly, it takes some time to set up settings, so often when teachers uses the hall, they use the "blank" version, with only steel boxes and so on to train their students. Near the arena, a storage with traning and spare robots and exoskeltons can be found, often used to train students. That, and some goggles that are to train agents to focus in different situations and to train them in getting the enemy closer...

    Fourth floor: Fourth floor is the boring floor. A lot of classrooms and so on, where math and other things like that, will be given to the students brains, wether they like it or not.

    Fifth floor and above: Who knows? It's all extremely restricted once you get here. But rumours say, that the second and third year students can be found up there...

    And thats that!
