The Night Wars
this rp is about a war between creatures of the night. basicaly, werewolves and vampires. (you can be a different race..but i have to approve of it. it cant be too far away from the story line.) No demons or fallens in this one guys, sorry. you can be a hybrid, but there are advantages and disadvantages. for instance:

werewolf-human hybrid: cant control werewolf form.
not as fast
stronger while in werewolf form
more vunerable to damage

vampire-Human hybrid: They can't flit (run really fast, as if the world drifts by. but they get really exhausted.)
They can go out in sunlight and still use their abilities
Thirst for blood is somehow much greater. (feed every day, possibly 2 or 3 times a day)
ablities arent quite as powerful


Vampire-werewolf hybrid:
DISADVANTAGES;
more vunerable to damage
need to drink a lot of blood (more than vampire human hybrids. although drinking blood can be substituted by eating wolfbane. [dont ask]) they can't flit
even the slightest upset in their minds
can cause the transformation
cant go out in sunlight
ADVANTAGES;
has both vampire and werewolf abilities
it has bat wings, so it can fly
can take more damage
stronger in wolf form
can turn into werewolf form whenever it wants (maybe more than it wants)


here are the regular pure-breed races advantages and disadvantages


Werewolf:

DISADVANTAGES;
can only turn into wolf form during night.
arent as smart in wolf form
have a horrible allergy to silver.
ADVANTAGES;
strength, speed, smell, and hearing are enhanced even in human form
while in wolf form, they become significantly more powerful
faster in wolf form



vampire:
DISADVANTAGES;
can only come out in sunlight for about an hour before being killed. (although you will almost instantly start sun-burning)
cant use they're abilities in daylight.
have to drink blood atleast once every week (although it will leave you weak, you must drink 3 times a week to be at full power.)

ADVANTAGES;
stronger than humans
faster than humans
perfect balance
can suck a person dry (you can use blood sucking as a weapon too)
most can fly
dont get sick or age.
flitting (this is where you run extremley fast, exact speed is unspecified)
fingernails (extremley sharp and thick)


alright thats it, now for the rules:

RULE 3: NO FLAMMING (yes i know this has been done a million times, but you still cant flam me)
